Time Flies

A game Developed in Godot as part of the Birds MiniJam.

The project took three days to develop with the team of 11 people. I managed the Art Team, creating all the assets from scratch.

Play as a bird! You do bird things... with a cost. Spend the little time you have to get to the exit using your bird abilities. Learning when to take it slow and when you can kill time by spending it is key to keeping your timer high and healthy.
