Pantheon of Shadows

A game Developed in Unity Engine as a part of a college assignment.

The project took two weeks to develop. A special thing about this project is that all the assets were developed by hand, avoiding the usage of any downloadable assets. Thanks to that, the project was optimised to weight only 5 Mb, inclusing textures, scripts and models.

The goal of the game is to collect shadow orbs, allowing the player to proceed further in the level while destroying obstacles and teleporting.
