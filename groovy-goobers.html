Groovy Goobers

A game Developed in Unity as the third-year college project.

The project was developed by three people as the final third-year project. I worked on the save systems and managed the development.

Groovy Goobers is a short and simple 4-key rhythm game developed by me and two of my college friends where you play as Marina, an up and coming dancer trying to rise the Disco Tower. The tower is populated by rival dancers who she'll have to defeat in dance battles to rise to the top!
